Minnan Technology Company is a global leader in power transmission, distribution equipment and power transformers series products. Main products include oil-immersed power distribution transformer, dry-type power distribution transformer, prefabricated substation, high & low voltage switchgear and high-voltage electrical components like capacitor, fuse, lightning arrester, vacuum circuit breaker, high-voltage isolation switch, vacuum load switch.
